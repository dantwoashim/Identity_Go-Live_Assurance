# Code of conduct

## Our standard

Treat other contributors with respect. Be direct, be fair, and leave room for people to learn.

We want this project to be a place where people can:

- ask basic questions without being talked down to
- disagree on technical choices without turning it personal
- contribute useful work without dealing with harassment or posturing

## Unacceptable behavior

This includes:

- harassment or intimidation
- personal attacks
- doxxing or threats
- sustained bad-faith disruption
- publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block participation when behavior crosses the line.
